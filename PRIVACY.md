# Privacy Policy for Piper App

**Last updated:** March 2026

Piper is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

## Information Collection

Piper **does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics** from users. All speech synthesis happens **locally on your device**, and no data is transmitted or stored externally.

## Data Use

Since Piper does not collect any data, there is **no use of personal information or usage analytics**.

## Third-Party Services

Piper does not rely on any external analytics or tracking services.
